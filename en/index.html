The Idea

The CoSy Festival is an art and music festival at Dornburg Castle near Magdeburg. CoSy is an acronym for "Contrasts in Symbiosis" and aims to merge contemporary music with modern art and culture in a historic building. On several stages and several hundred square meters of exhibition space in the castle and the associated castle park, professionals and up-and-coming artists will be given the opportunity to present their music and art. In cooperation with local and national associations and collectives, we want to highlight contrasts, be it a young cultural scene in an old cultural heritage site, technological modernity with ecological sustainability or the tranquillity of the rural region with innovation from the vibrant city. We want to show possibilities and ways in which these can be brought into symbiotic harmony.
